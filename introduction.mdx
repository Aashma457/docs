## Get Up and Running in Four Steps

New to ORArchive? Follow these steps in order and you'll have a fully configured archive workspace ready to use in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Head to [app.orachive.com/register](https://app.orachive.com/register) and sign up with your work email. Once you submit the form, ORArchive sends a verification email to confirm your address. Check your inbox and click the confirmation link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Archive Workspace">
    After verifying your email, complete your profile by adding your organization name and job title. Then choose a unique workspace name — this becomes the identifier for your archive (for example, `acme-corp`). Your workspace is the top-level container for all the records your team will manage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Your First Records">
    With your workspace ready, click the **Upload** button in the top bar or drag files directly onto the main content area. ORArchive accepts PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PNG, JPG, MP4, and many other common formats. After upload, add a title, description, tags, and a category so the record is easy to find later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Organize and Share">
    Move records into folders, apply tags, and use collections to group related content. When you're ready to collaborate, open any record, click **Share**, and invite a colleague by email. Assign them a **View** or **Edit** permission level to control what they can do with the record.
  </Step>
</Steps>
