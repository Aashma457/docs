Working with records one at a time is fine for small tasks, but when you need to reorganize a folder, apply a new tag scheme across dozens of files, or back up a project's worth of documents, bulk operations save you significant time. ORArchive lets you select any number of records at once and apply a shared action to all of them in a single step, with each change logged individually per record for full traceability.

## Selecting Multiple Records

To enter bulk selection mode, hover over any record in the content area — a **checkbox** appears on the left side of the record row. Click the checkbox to select that record. Once at least one record is selected, checkboxes become permanently visible on all records in the current view, making it easy to expand your selection.

Use the following shortcuts to speed up selection:

- **Click a checkbox** — select or deselect an individual record
- **Shift+click** — select all records between the last selected record and the one you just clicked, inclusive
- **Ctrl+A** (Windows/Linux) or **Cmd+A** (macOS) — select every record currently displayed on the page

As you select records, a **bulk action toolbar** appears at the bottom of the screen. The toolbar shows a count of how many records are currently selected (e.g., *14 records selected*) and displays buttons for each available bulk action. Click the × in the toolbar to clear your selection and exit bulk mode.

## Available Bulk Actions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Move to Folder">
    Relocate all selected records to a different folder in your workspace. Clicking **Move to Folder** in the bulk action toolbar opens a folder picker showing your full folder tree. Navigate to the destination folder and click **Move**. All selected records are moved simultaneously; their metadata and version histories are unaffected.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Add Tags">
    Apply one or more tags to every record in your selection at once. Click **Add Tags** in the toolbar, type each tag in the input field (pressing Enter or comma after each one), and click **Apply**. Tags are **appended** to each record — any tags the records already have are preserved. This means you can safely run this action on a mixed selection without worrying about overwriting existing tag data.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Change Category">
    Reassign all selected records to a single category. Click **Change Category**, choose the target category from the dropdown, and click **Apply**. This action **overrides** the existing category on every selected record, replacing whatever each record was previously assigned to. Use this action carefully on mixed selections — see the warning in [Editing Records](/records/editing-records) about the impact on saved filtered views.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Share">
    Open the share dialog for your entire selection at once. Any permission settings you configure in the dialog — view-only, comment, edit, or a specific user or group — are applied uniformly to all selected records. This is particularly useful when onboarding a new team member who needs access to a set of related project documents at once.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Export">
    Download all selected records as a single **ZIP archive**. Click **Export** in the bulk action toolbar and ORArchive begins preparing the download in the background. The ZIP file contains the original file for each selected record, organized in folders that mirror your workspace structure. It also includes a **CSV manifest** listing each record's title, description, category, tags, creation date, and last-modified date, giving you a structured index of the exported content. You will receive a notification (and a download link) when the ZIP is ready.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Move to Trash">
    Send all selected records to the Trash folder in one action. Click **Move to Trash** in the toolbar and confirm the prompt. All selected records are removed from their current locations and appear in Trash, where they follow the standard 30-day auto-deletion policy. See [Deleting Records](/records/deleting-records) for details on restoring or permanently removing trashed records.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Tip>
  Use the **Export** bulk action to create periodic offline backups of your most important records. Select a critical project folder, export it as a ZIP, and store the archive in a secure location outside ORArchive. The included CSV manifest means you retain a readable index of the records even if you open the backup years later.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Every bulk operation is recorded in the **Activity Feed** on a per-record basis — not as a single batch entry. This means you can audit exactly which records were affected, who performed the action, and when, even when hundreds of records were modified in a single bulk operation.
</Note>
