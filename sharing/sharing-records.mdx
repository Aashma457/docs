Sharing records in ORArchive lets your team collaborate on the documents that matter most. You can share individual records or entire collections with any teammate in your workspace, control exactly what they can do with that content, and revoke access at any time — all without leaving the platform.

## Sharing a record with a team member

When you want to give a colleague access to a specific record, use the share dialog in the record's detail panel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the record">
    Click the record in your archive. The detail panel slides in from the right side of the screen, displaying the record's metadata, preview, and action buttons.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the Share button">
    In the panel header, click the **Share** button (the person+ icon). The share dialog opens as an overlay.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the team member's email address">
    Type the team member's email address in the **Invite people** field. ORArchive automatically suggests users already in your workspace as you type — select a suggestion to fill the field instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a permission level">
    Choose either **Viewer** or **Editor** from the permission dropdown next to the email field:
    - **Viewer** — the recipient can view and download the record but cannot change its metadata.
    - **Editor** — the recipient can view, download, and edit the record's metadata and tags.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an optional note">
    Optionally, type a short message in the **Add a note** field. This message is included in the invitation email so the recipient understands the context of the share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Send Invite">
    Click **Send Invite** to confirm. ORArchive immediately sends the recipient an email notification containing a direct link to the record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you need to share several related records at once, consider grouping them into a collection first. See [Organizing with Collections](/organizing/collections) for a faster workflow.
</Tip>

## Sharing a collection

Sharing a collection works exactly the same way as sharing an individual record, but it grants access to every record the collection contains under a single permission level.

Open the collection, click its **Share** button (person+ icon) in the collection header, and follow the same steps above. Any records subsequently added to the collection are automatically accessible to everyone who already has a share on it.

## Viewing who has access

To see a full list of everyone who can currently access a record:

1. Open the record and click **Share** to open the share dialog.
2. Scroll to the **People with access** section at the bottom of the dialog.

Each entry shows the user's name, email address, their assigned permission level, and the date access was originally granted. This gives you an at-a-glance audit trail without leaving the record.

## Changing permissions

You can update a collaborator's permission level at any time after the initial share.

In the **People with access** section of the share dialog, click the **permission badge** (e.g., *Viewer* or *Editor*) displayed next to the user's name. A small dropdown appears — select the new permission level. The change takes effect immediately the next time the user accesses the record.

## Revoking access

To remove a person's access to a record entirely:

1. Open the share dialog for the record.
2. In the **People with access** section, click the **×** button to the right of the user's name.
3. Their access is removed instantly. They will no longer be able to view, search for, or download the record through ORArchive.

<Warning>
  Revoking access only prevents future access within ORArchive. It does **not** invalidate or delete any copies of files the user may have already downloaded to their own device. For sensitive documents, review what was accessed in the record's **Activity** tab before revoking.
</Warning>
