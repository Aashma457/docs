ORArchive's notification system keeps you informed about activity that matters to you — whether a collaborator edits a record you own, someone leaves a comment, or your workspace is approaching its storage limit. Notifications surface in two places: the **bell icon** in the top-right navigation bar delivers in-app alerts in real time, and **email notifications** land in the inbox associated with your account. You can tune both channels independently for each notification type.

---

## Notification Types

The table below lists every notification type available in ORArchive, what triggers it, and whether it is turned on by default for new accounts.

| Notification | Trigger | Default |
|---|---|---|
| **Record shared with you** | Someone shares a record or collection directly with you | On |
| **Record edited** | A record you own is modified by a collaborator | On |
| **Comment added** | A comment is posted on a record you own or follow | On |
| **Upload complete** | A large file finishes processing after upload | On |
| **Storage warning** | Your workspace reaches 80% of its storage capacity | On |
| **Link accessed** | Someone opens a shareable link you created | Off |
| **Weekly digest** | A weekly summary of all workspace activity | Off |

## Configuring Notifications

You can enable or disable any notification type — for email, in-app, or both — from the Notifications settings page. Changes take effect immediately and save automatically without requiring a page refresh.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Notification Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Notifications** using the avatar menu in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust each notification type">
    For each notification listed, use the **Email** and **In-App** toggle switches to turn that channel on or off independently. For example, you can receive an in-app alert for comments without getting an email for each one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your preferences are saved">
    Toggles save automatically as you switch them. No additional save action is required — a brief confirmation indicator appears next to each toggle when a change is recorded.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## In-App Notification Center

The in-app notification center gives you an at-a-glance view of recent activity without leaving ORArchive. Click the **bell icon** (🔔) in the top navigation bar to open the notification panel.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Unread Indicators" icon="circle-dot">
    Unread notifications are marked with a **blue dot** on the left side. The bell icon itself displays a badge count when you have unread items.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Jumping to a Record" icon="arrow-right">
    Click any notification in the panel to navigate directly to the relevant record, comment thread, or activity that triggered it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mark All as Read" icon="check">
    Click **Mark all as read** at the top of the panel to clear the badge counter and remove all blue dot indicators at once.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Notification History" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    The panel retains your last 100 notifications. Older entries are automatically archived and no longer appear in the panel.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Email Notification Frequency

For each notification type that has email delivery enabled, you can choose how frequently those emails are sent. This lets you stay informed without committing to a constant stream of individual messages.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Immediate">
    An email is sent as soon as the triggering event occurs. Best for high-priority notifications like **Record shared with you** or **Storage warning**, where prompt awareness matters.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Hourly Digest">
    All events of that type within the past hour are bundled into a single email. A good middle ground for active collaborators who want regular updates without per-event emails.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Daily Digest">
    All events of that type from the previous 24 hours are summarized in one email, delivered each morning. Ideal for lower-urgency notifications such as **Comment added** or **Record edited**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Off">
    No emails are sent for that notification type regardless of activity. You can still receive in-app alerts if the In-App toggle remains enabled.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
  If your workspace sees a high volume of edits and comments each day, switch those notification types to **Daily Digest** to avoid inbox overload. You'll still get a complete summary every morning without dozens of individual emails throughout the day.
</Tip>

## Unsubscribing from a Specific Record

If you're following a particularly active record and no longer want notifications from it, you can unfollow it without changing your global notification preferences. This is useful when you've been tagged in a discussion you've since resolved or a record you only needed to review temporarily.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the record">
    Navigate to the record you want to stop following and open its detail view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the options menu">
    Click the **⋯** (more options) menu in the top-right corner of the record detail panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Unfollow">
    Click **Unfollow** from the dropdown menu. You will immediately stop receiving notifications for that specific record. Your global notification preferences for other records remain unchanged.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  To start following a record again after unfollowing it, open the same **⋯** menu and select **Follow**. You'll resume receiving notifications for that record based on your current global settings.
</Info>
