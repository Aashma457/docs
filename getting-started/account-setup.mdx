Before you can upload records or collaborate with teammates, you need an active ORArchive account and a configured workspace. The setup process takes only a few minutes: you'll register with your work email, verify your identity, fill in a short profile, and choose a workspace name that your whole organization will use. Follow the steps below to get everything in place.

<Note>
  ORArchive requires passwords to be **at least 8 characters long** and include at least **one uppercase letter** and **one number**. Passwords that don't meet these requirements will be rejected at the registration step. Consider using a password manager to generate and store a strong password securely.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Sign up with your **work email address** rather than a personal one. ORArchive uses your email domain to enable team collaboration features — colleagues who register with the same domain can be added to your workspace more quickly, and domain-level sharing policies apply automatically.
</Tip>

## Creating Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register at the Sign-Up Page">
    Open your browser and navigate to [app.orachive.com/register](https://app.orachive.com/register). On the registration form, enter your **full name**, **work email address**, and a **secure password** that meets the requirements noted above. Review the terms of service, then click **Create Account** to submit your details. ORArchive creates a pending account and immediately sends a verification email to the address you provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email Address">
    Open your email inbox and look for a message from ORArchive with the subject line **"Confirm your ORArchive email"**. Inside the email, click the **Confirm Email** button. This link is valid for **24 hours** — if it expires before you click it, return to the login page and request a new verification email using the **Resend confirmation** link. Once confirmed, your browser redirects you automatically to the profile setup screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    On the profile setup screen, enter your **organization name** and **job title**. These details appear on your account and help teammates identify you when records are shared. You can also upload a **profile photo** at this stage, though this is optional and can be added or changed at any time from your account settings. Click **Continue** when you're ready to move on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Workspace Name">
    Enter a **workspace name** for your archive. This name becomes the unique identifier used in your archive's URL and internal references — for example, entering `acme-corp` results in a workspace accessible at `app.orachive.com/acme-corp`. Choose something short, recognizable, and free of spaces (use hyphens instead). Once you're satisfied with the name, click **Finish Setup**. ORArchive provisions your workspace and takes you to your new, empty archive dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logging In and Resetting Your Password

Once your account is active, log in at [app.orachive.com/login](https://app.orachive.com/login). Enter the email address and password you registered with, then click **Sign In**. ORArchive drops you into your workspace dashboard immediately.

If you forget your password, click **Forgot your password?** on the login page. Enter your registered email address and click **Send Reset Link**. You'll receive an email containing a secure link that lets you choose a new password. The reset link expires after **1 hour** — if it times out, you can request another from the same forgot-password page.

<Warning>
  Password reset links are single-use and expire after one hour. If you requested a reset but didn't receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder. If the email still doesn't arrive, contact your IT administrator to confirm that emails from `noreply@orachive.com` are not being blocked by your organization's mail filter.
</Warning>
