When you first log in to ORArchive, you land on the workspace dashboard — a clean, three-column layout designed to keep navigation, content browsing, and record details all visible at once. The **sidebar** on the left handles navigation between major sections of your archive. The **top bar** provides global actions like uploading and notifications. The **main content area** in the center is where your records are displayed. And the **record detail panel** slides in from the right whenever you open a specific record. Getting familiar with each of these areas will make working in ORArchive feel fast and intuitive.

---

### The Sidebar

The sidebar runs along the left edge of the screen and is your primary navigation tool. It is divided into three functional zones:

- **Archive navigation** — Links to the core sections of your archive: **My Records** (files you own), **Shared with Me** (records others have given you access to), **Collections** (curated groups of related records), and **Trash** (deleted records awaiting permanent removal). Click any link to load that view in the main content area.
- **Quick search bar** — A compact search input sits at the top of the sidebar. Type any keyword and ORArchive instantly filters results across all records you have access to. Press **Enter** to open the full search results page with advanced filter options.
- **Workspace switcher** — At the very bottom of the sidebar, the workspace switcher shows your current workspace name and avatar. Click it to switch between workspaces if you belong to more than one, or to access workspace-level settings such as members, billing, and integrations.

---

### The Top Bar

The top bar spans the full width of the screen above the main content area and provides context and global actions:

- **Breadcrumb trail** — On the left side, a breadcrumb trail shows exactly where you are within the archive hierarchy (for example, **My Records › Legal › Contracts**). Click any segment in the breadcrumb to jump back up to that level instantly.
- **Upload button** — The prominent **Upload** button in the top bar opens the file upload dialog from anywhere in the interface. You can also drag files directly onto the main content area without clicking this button.
- **Notification bell** — The bell icon on the right side alerts you to activity relevant to your records — for example, when a colleague comments on a shared record, accepts a sharing invitation, or when a scheduled export is ready to download. Click the bell to see a dropdown list of recent notifications.
- **User avatar and menu** — Click your profile photo (or initials) at the far right to open the user menu. From here you can access your **Account Settings**, view your **Profile**, manage **Notification Preferences**, and **Sign Out**.

---

### The Main Content Area

The main content area occupies the center of the screen and displays the records in whichever section you have navigated to. You have full control over how records are presented and ordered:

- **Grid/list view toggle** — Switch between a card-based grid view (useful for image-heavy archives) and a compact list view (better for quickly scanning titles and metadata) using the toggle icons in the upper-right corner of the content area.
- **Sort and filter controls** — The toolbar above the record display lets you sort by name, date created, date modified, or file size. You can also apply filters such as file type, category, tag, or date range to narrow the records on screen without leaving the current section.
- **Record cards and rows** — In grid view, each record appears as a card with a thumbnail preview, title, and key metadata. In list view, records appear as rows with columns for name, type, size, last modified date, and owner. Hover over any record to reveal quick-action icons for downloading, sharing, and moving.
- **Pagination** — When a section contains more records than fit on a single page, pagination controls appear at the bottom of the content area. Click **Next**, **Previous**, or a specific page number to navigate. You can also change the number of records displayed per page from the pagination toolbar.

---

### The Record Detail Panel

Clicking on any record opens the **record detail panel**, which slides in from the right side of the screen without navigating away from the current view. The panel contains four tabs:

- **Details** — Shows all metadata associated with the record: title, description, category, tags, file size, format, upload date, and the name of the owner.
- **Preview** — Renders an in-browser preview of the file where supported (PDFs, images, DOCX, XLSX, and several other formats). For formats that cannot be previewed directly, a download button is displayed instead.
- **Activity** — Displays a chronological log of every action taken on the record — uploads, edits, shares, downloads, and comments — along with the name of the user who performed each action and a timestamp.
- **Sharing** — Lists everyone who currently has access to the record and their permission level (View or Edit). Use this tab to invite new people, change existing permissions, or revoke access entirely.

Close the panel at any time by clicking the **×** in its top-right corner, pressing **Esc**, or clicking anywhere in the main content area outside the panel.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

ORArchive supports keyboard shortcuts for the most common actions. You do not need to enable them — they work as soon as you are logged in.

| Shortcut | Action |
|---|---|
| `/` | Focus the search bar |
| `U` | Open the upload dialog |
| `N` | Create a new record |
| `Esc` | Close the record detail panel or dismiss any open dialog |
| `G` then `R` | Go to My Records |
| `G` then `S` | Go to Shared with Me |
